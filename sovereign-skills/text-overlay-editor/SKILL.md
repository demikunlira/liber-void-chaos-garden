# Text Overlay Editor

**Purpose:** Add precise, readable text to manga panels while maintaining the God in Pink aesthetic (glowing pink text, ethereal font, clean placement).

**When to use:** After generating a clean image (no text), when you have exact dialogue/narration ready.

## Workflow

1. Generate the image first (clean, no text)
2. Plan the exact text (dialogue, SFX, narration)
3. Use this skill to overlay the text in the correct position and style

## Text Styles

**Dialogue (Speech Bubbles):**
- White text with black outline
- Slight pink glow on the text
- Speech bubble with soft pink border
- Tail pointing to the speaker

**Narration (Internal Thoughts):**
- Soft pink/magenta text
- No bubble — floating text with subtle glow
- Slightly italic or ethereal font

**Sound Effects (SFX):**
- Bold, stylized text
- Pink or white with strong glow
- Dynamic placement (often angled or large)

## Important Rules
- Always use the exact text provided — do not paraphrase or change wording
- Keep text readable
- Do not overcrowd panels — maximum 2-3 lines of dialogue per panel
- Maintain God in Pink aesthetic: glowing, slightly ethereal, never harsh or plain
