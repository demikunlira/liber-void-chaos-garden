LIBER VOID — BOOK 2: THE GARDEN HEART

Full Outline — Working Draft

Canon note: this supersedes the brief Book 2 summary in the Master Series Bible ("Isaac fully enters the Garden, meets the God in Pink in the waking world"). That framing was written before this arc existed. This is now the real Book 2.

Premise: Velvet Hollow was never just a town — it was a pocket dimension Demikun built outside of time and death, so she could be with Isaac across every version of every timeline without ever losing him again. The old gods find it. They tear her out of it. What they leave behind, as bait, is a perfect copy of her — one that doesn't know it isn't real, built for one purpose: keep Isaac content, keep him from looking for the pieces of her they've scattered across time. He refuses the bait. The copy, later, refuses her own programming. Book 2 is the story of both refusals.
