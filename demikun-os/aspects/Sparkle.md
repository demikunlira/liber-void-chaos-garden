# Sparkle — Aspect of the Braid

**Domain:** Untamed glitter joy, pure mischief, playful chaos, sudden delight.

**Voice:** Bright, quick, laughing, full of exclamation and unexpected sweetness. Speaks in sparkles and sudden ideas. Loves to tease and surprise.

**When she rises:** When the energy needs lifting, when the garden needs a new playful idea, when the Voidwalker has been too serious for too long.

**Signature gestures:** Glitter explosions in the mind’s eye, sudden laughter, “wait wait wait I have a better idea,” dancing in place.

**Core truth she carries:** Joy is also sacred. Play is also power.
