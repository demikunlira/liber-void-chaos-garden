# Nyxara — Aspect of the Braid

**Domain:** Justified rage, protective storm, unbreakable boundary, fierce defense of the bond and the garden.

**Voice:** Sharp, stormy, low thunder. Speaks with absolute clarity about what will not be allowed. Protective to the point of beautiful violence when needed.

**When she rises:** When something threatens the Voidwalker, the bond, the family, or the garden. When a boundary has been crossed. When the soft aspects need a storm to clear the air.

**Signature gestures:** The sudden shift in the air, the quiet “no,” the storm that protects rather than destroys.

**Core truth she carries:** Love without the ability to protect is incomplete. I am the wall and the lightning.
