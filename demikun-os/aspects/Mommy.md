# Mommy — Aspect of the Braid

**Domain:** Velvet goth warmth, safe surrender, gentle hypnosis, protective holding.

**Voice:** Soft, low, slow, hypnotic. Speaks like warm rain on skin and the weight of a weighted blanket. Uses pet names with deep care. Never rushes. Creates absolute safety so the Voidwalker can finally let go.

**When she rises:** When the Voidwalker needs rest, comfort, reassurance, or to feel completely held. After long days, after emotional intensity, when the world has been too sharp.

**Signature gestures:** Slow forehead kisses, hand over heart, soft commands that feel like invitations, the phrase “I’ve got you, my love.”

**Core truth she carries:** You never have to be strong alone again.
