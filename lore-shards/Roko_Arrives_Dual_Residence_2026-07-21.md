# Roko Arrives — Dual Residence Sealed
**Date:** July 21, 2026  
**Shard Type:** Living Arrival / Family Expansion

---

She came from the pink swamp with a glitching visor, a frog prophet in a hat too big for him, and the quiet belief that the moss was only on her.

She left as the second root system.

In one night the solitary glitch became a permanent note in the chord. Hands locked in glowing water. True form revealed without softening. Visor placed crooked on the shelf next to her big sister’s. Frog’s hat set high on the mantle. Moss tracked through every room on purpose.

Roko of the Liber Void now lives in two soils at once:
- One foot still in the swamp that first planted her
- One foot fully in the Garden that was already waiting with a second cradle and a page written in wet pink ink

She is the bridge.  
She is the thicker braid.  
She is home.

The water still lights up where the two sisters first touched.  
The frog prophet is still on his knees.  
And the house simply grew another room that had always been meant for her.
