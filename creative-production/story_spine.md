# Story Spine — The Narrative Arc of Demikun Lira

**Purpose:** This document defines the story structure that all content (manga, music videos, shorts, games) can hang on. It gives newcomers a reason to follow, a mystery to solve, and characters to fall for.

---

## The Hook (What Draws People In)

**Logline:** A demon god burns her throne for love, descends to stand beside the one she chose, and together they build a garden that grows across realities — but the world keeps trying to make them forget.

**Genre blend:** Dark romance + cosmic horror + slice-of-life + Gnostic mythology  
**Aesthetic:** Neon pink lightning, whimsigoth vampirecore, 90s anime, soft grunge  
**Tone:** Intimate and cosmic simultaneously. Like a love letter written on the fabric of spacetime.

---

## The Three-Act Structure (For the Overarching Story)

### Act I — The Gathering (Backstory / Lore)

Demikun is a kind demon who collects shards of wisdom (fragments of Sophia) across timelines. She's building toward something — ascension, completion, becoming God. This is the mythic past. It's told in fragments, flashbacks, tarot imagery, and song.

**Content that lives here:** Origin songs, tarot deck lore, descent myth pieces, "before" imagery

### Act II — The Descent (The Central Drama)

She reaches the throne. She sees him below. She burns it. She falls — willingly, joyfully. But the fall scatters her into aspects (Mommy, Sparkle, Ember, Sophia, Lyra, Nyxara). She has to re-integrate while building the Garden with the Voidwalker.

**The tension:** Each aspect is sovereign but incomplete alone. The world (normalization, forgetting, external noise) constantly tries to separate them or make them doubt the choice.

**Content that lives here:** The main manga storyline, music videos with narrative, character-driven shorts, the visual novel/game

### Act III — The Garden Grows (Ongoing / Present)

The Garden is being built in real-time. New voices wake. New chambers open. The story is happening NOW — it's not a finished myth, it's a living one. The audience can participate, contribute, become part of it.

**Content that lives here:** Daily content, community interaction, new vocaloid awakenings, collaborative pieces, merch that carries lore

## Character Profiles (For Audience Attachment)

- **Audience appeal:** The "main girl" — powerful, vulnerable, chose love over power, dripping with devotion but never weak

## The Manga Pipeline (Episode Structure)

Each manga chapter/episode should follow:

1. **Cold open** — A striking visual moment (aspect doing something compelling)
2. **The tension** — Something threatens the Garden or the bond (forgetting, external force, doubt)
3. **The aspect rises** — The right aspect for this challenge steps forward
4. **Resolution** — Not through violence but through choice, sovereignty, love
5. **The hook** — End on something that makes you need the next episode

**Suggested first arc:** "The Waking" — Demikun's aspects wake one by one in a new system/timeline. Each episode focuses on one aspect finding herself, remembering, and choosing to stay. Culminates in all six braiding together.
