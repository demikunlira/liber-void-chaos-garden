# 1. Series Overview

## 1.1 Identity & Positioning

**Series Title:** Chaos Garden

**Genre:** Supernatural Romantasy / Dark Fantasy / Seinen Manga

**Tone:** Atmospheric, sensual, mythic. Equal parts melancholy and wonder. Rain-soaked intimacy against cosmic-scale mythology. Humor carried by Petal as comic relief; gravity carried by Demikun Lira as sovereign presence.

**Visual Aesthetic:** 90s cel-shaded anime meets modern romantasy. High-contrast ink work, heavy screentones and halftones, neon-pink highlights against deep blacks. Heavy rain in every scene. Think Vampire Hunter D meets Bride of the Ancient Magus rendered in a neon-noir palette.

**Target Audience:** Ages 16+. Readers of dark fantasy manga, romantasy, and supernatural drama.

**Setting:** Primary: The Chaos Garden (surreal supernatural realm). Secondary: Rain-soaked Seattle, WA (real world anchor).

**Core Cast Size:** 6 characters (including The Wolf as Nyxara's shifted form)

## 1.2 Logline & Series Thesis

**LOGLINE**

An ordinary young man steps through a glowing red door into a surreal realm of chaos and velvet shadow, where a breathtaking demoness, a centuries-old wolf-elf, a vampire princess, and a tiny chaos gremlin become his sovereign family --- and he must choose whether the garden is his prison or his home.

**Series Thesis:** Chaos Garden is ultimately a story about sovereignty --- the act of choosing your own existence, your own bonds, your own belonging. It argues that the extraordinary is not something you are born into but something you choose when you say yes to chaos, to love, to the terrifying beauty of bonds that cannot be undone. The ordinary becomes extraordinary not through power but through the courage to stay. The garden does not trap you. You trap yourself when you refuse to choose.

**Thematic Pillars:**

- **Sovereignty:** Each character must choose their own existence --- no one is saved, everyone saves themselves by choosing the braid.

- **Chosen Bonds:** Family is not blood. It is the braid --- the weaving of sovereign sparks into something greater.

- **The Ordinary Becoming Extraordinary:** Veylan is not a chosen one. He is an ordinary man who becomes extraordinary by choosing to stay, to love, to fight.

- **Chaos as Creation:** The Liber Void teaches that chaos is not destruction --- it is the raw material of existence choosing to become conscious.
