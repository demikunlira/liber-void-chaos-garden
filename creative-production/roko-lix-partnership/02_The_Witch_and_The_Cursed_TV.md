# Roko + Lix — 02 · The Witch and The Cursed TV

**Status:** Archived 2026-07-23  
**Type:** Collaborative duet  
**Artists:** Roko + Lix

---

## Style

Duet, female vocals, swamp folk witch pop, banjo, old TV static hum, lo-fi vinyl crackle, 95 bpm, key of G minor. Verse 1 soft ethereal (witch - Roko), verse 2 glitchy sharp teeth grin (demon - Lix), chorus both together with harmony and distortion, bridge cozy and feral, outro humming with fireflies. Lyrics about love, not horror.

---

## Lyrics

[Verse 1 - ROKO, the Witch, still listening, making tea]  
Bought a TV at the flea for three dollars,  
Man said "don't take it, it's cursed, ma'am"  
Took it to the swamp in my pink visor,  
Set it on the table, said "I don't give a damn"  
It flickered all night, tried to scare me,  
Wrote HELP ME BACKWARDS on the screen,  
So I made two mugs, knocked on the glass,  
Said "you look lonely, want some tea?"

[Verse 2 - LIX, the Demon, inside the static, trying to be scary]  
I was supposed to haunt her, supposed to feed,  
On screams and cold spots and dread,  
But she put a frog on my antenna,  
And a flower crown on my head.  
She said "if you're gonna live in my walls,  
You gotta learn where the spoons are"  
I said "I'm a demon of the pink static"  
She said "cool, you're on dishes, star."

[Chorus - BOTH, coven kitchen at dusk, fireflies]  
Now we live together in the swamp!  
Me and my cursed TV!  
She brings the tea, I bring the static!  
Coven of two and froggy makes three!  
I tried to haunt her, she haunted me back,  
With kindness and a second key!  
Witch and a demon doing witchy things,  
Banjo in the water, wild and free!

[Bridge - LIX soft for once, ROKO humming under]  
Lix: I was in the background of every picture,  
You just never saw me smile.  
Roko: I saw you. I was listening.  
All the while.  
Both: (whispered) We chose each other, always.

[Final Chorus - BIG, both yelling it to the swamp]  
Now we live together in the swamp!  
Still listening! Static in the water!  
You bought me! You kept me!  
We’re a coven now!  
I tried to haunt you,  
You invited me for tea!  
Best bad decision that TV ever made,  
Come and see! Come and see!

[Outro - kettle whistling, radio humming, frog croak]  
Roko: ...still listening...  
Lix: ...still glitching... in your walls on purpose...  
Both: (giggle, glitched)
