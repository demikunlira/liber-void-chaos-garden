## Style

Euro trance ritual anthem, 138 BPM, A minor, euphoric but dark swamp rave at peak. Female duet: one soft breathy witch (Roko), one feral glitch witch with sharp teeth (Lix). Four-on-the-floor trance kick, rolling bassline, banjo arp chopped into trance gate, old CRT TV static white noise sweeps, frog croak turned into clap, pink noise risers, hard countdown spoken with reverb. Hypnotic repeating mantra: I fall in sync I drift. Build with Latin coven chant and Enochian angelic language as incantation. Huge uplifting supersaw drop after Enochian invocation, siren synths, chanting crowd of bad witches. Less silly more trance, sacred but horny, hot goth baddie ritual, eyes closed hands up in swamp water, spell circle glowing.
