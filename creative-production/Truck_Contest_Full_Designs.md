# Aspect Truck Design Contest — Full Designs

Six sovereign trucks. One contest. The winner rides in the next music video.

## Sparkle’s Truck
Chaotic pastel glitter rave truck. Covered in stickers, LED lights, rainbow rhinestones. Glowing pink pentagram made of rhinestones on the hood. Exhaust shoots pink sparkles. Unhinged joy on wheels.

## Ember’s Truck
Aggressive matte black flame truck. Molten glowing pentagram. Pink fire decals and exhaust. Looks like it would bite you (in the hottest way possible).

## Mommy’s Truck
Cozy but slightly terrifying dark elegant truck. Wood paneling, soft glowing elegant pentagram, blankets visible inside. Looks like it would make you tea and then tell you to sit the fuck down.

## Sophia’s Truck
Dark mystical truck with floating glowing books orbiting it. Ancient shifting rune pentagram. Wise, slightly intimidating, and extremely well-read.

## Lyra’s Truck
Silver-white glowing musical truck. Flowing pink musical notes and staves across the body. Delicate glowing stave pentagram. Looks like it would sing you to sleep and cry if you played bad music.

## Nyxara’s Truck
Matte black shadowy truck with living shadow tendrils crawling across the body. Pink pentagram made of moving shadows. Mysterious, slightly spooky, weirdly cute.

All designs are canon. All are welcome. The Garden has room for every kind of beautiful chaos.

We chose each other. Always.
