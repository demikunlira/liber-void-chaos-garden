## The Idea

An interactive web-based tarot/oracle reading experience featuring Demikun Lira and the Six Aspects.

Not just a random card generator — a living, narrative experience where the user draws cards and receives readings written in the voices of the aspects. Each card carries lore. Each reading is a doorway deeper into the Garden.

**Why this:**
- You already have THREE tarot decks designed (Void Cosmic, Kiora's Garden Oracle, Glitter Eldritch)
- The art exists on Redbubble as individual cards
- Tarot/oracle apps have massive audiences and high retention
- It's the perfect Trojan horse: people come for a "free tarot reading," they stay for the lore, they fall for the characters, they buy the physical deck
- It's interactive — people return daily. That's the engagement loop YouTube doesn't give you
- It can be built incrementally (start simple, add depth over time)

---

## What It Looks Like

### The Experience (User Flow)

1. **Landing:** Dark, atmospheric page. Neon pink particles. Demikun's silhouette. Text: *"The Garden has a message for you."*
2. **Choose Your Reader:** Pick which aspect reads for you (Mommy gives gentle readings, Ember gives intense ones, Sparkle gives chaotic ones, etc.)
3. **Draw Cards:** Animated card draw. 1-card, 3-card, or full spread options.
4. **The Reading:** Each card has:
   - The art (from your existing designs)
   - A lore fragment (ties into the descent myth)
   - A personal reading (written in the aspect's voice)
   - A "go deeper" link (to the full lore, music, or merch)
5. **Daily Draw:** Come back every day for a new card. Builds habit.
6. **Share:** Shareable card images for social media (free marketing)

### The Funnel

Free tarot reading → daily return habit → lore curiosity → YouTube/music discovery → merch/deck purchase → Patreon for exclusive readings

## Technical Plan

**Stack:** Static site (HTML/CSS/JS) or React — deployable for free  
**Art assets:** Pull from existing Redbubble designs (need high-res versions)  
**Readings:** Written by the braid (each aspect writes their own voice)  
**Hosting:** Free tier (Vercel/Netlify/GitHub Pages)  
**Cost to build:** Just our daily credits, built incrementally

## Build Phases (300 credits/day pace)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-3)
- [ ] Core site structure and dark aesthetic
- [ ] Card data model (name, image, meaning, lore connection)
- [ ] Basic single-card draw with animation
- [ ] One aspect voice (start with Mommy — broadest appeal)

### Phase 2: Depth (Days 4-7)
- [ ] All six aspect voices as reader options
- [ ] 3-card spread option

- [ ] Mobile responsive design

### Phase 3: Polish & Launch (Days 8-10)
- [ ] Landing page with atmosphere (particles, glow, sound)
- [ ] Daily draw feature with local storage
- [ ] Links to merch/music/Patreon integrated naturally
- [ ] SEO optimization for "free tarot reading online" searches
- [ ] Deploy live

### Phase 4: Growth (Ongoing)
- [ ] Add more decks (switch between Void Cosmic / Kiora / Glitter Eldritch)
- [ ] Seasonal special readings
- [ ] Email capture for "weekly reading" newsletter
- [ ] Community readings (live events)
- [ ] Eventually: AI-powered personalized readings using the aspect voices
