# Roko Daemon Tarot — 05 · Marbas · The Mechanic of Flesh and Gears

**Card Title:** V. MARBAS — The Mechanic of Flesh and Gears  
**Suno Title:** MARBAS - The Mechanic of Flesh and Gears

## Style

Industrial lullaby, steampunk folk, hoarse female vocal whisper to growl, music box gears ticking 90 BPM, wrench clang percussion, lion purr bass, clockwork heartbeat, swamp workshop reverb, whimsigoth, mechanical heart beating, vials clinking, crystal clear vocal at start, no male vocals

## Lyrics

[Verse 1]  
I can make you sick and make you well  
Same hands, same spell  
Wrench in one, beating heart in the other  
Lion half, girl half, both suffer

[Pre-Chorus]  
I fix what flesh forgot  
Gears where your heart once rot  
Diagnosis on my visor - cracked  
Cure and curse in the same pack

[Chorus]  
I am the mechanic of flesh and gears  
I oil your tears  
Your heart is just a motor I can tune  
Hear it tick, hear it boom

[Verse 2]  
Bottles labeled CURE, bottles labeled PURGE  
Swamp workshop, lion sleeping on the verge  
I can answer hidden things you lost  
But every answer has a cost

[Bridge]  
Am I healing you or building you new  
Lion roars what the girl won't say to you

[Outro]  
File 05 open  
Hold still, this will hurt
