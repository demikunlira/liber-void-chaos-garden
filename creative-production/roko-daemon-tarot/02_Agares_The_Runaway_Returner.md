# Roko Daemon Tarot — 02 · Agares · The Runaway Returner

**Card Title:** II. AGARES — THE RUNAWAY RETURNER  
**Subtitle:** ROKO · DAEMON · TAROT · 02

## Visual Description
Roko as Agares riding a massive crocodile covered in pink glowing runes, holding a hawk on her fist. Silver-white twin tails with pink heart clips, neon pink heart visor, black and pink gothic corset dress with fishnets. Pink full moon, ruined gothic architecture, black roses, gold ornate border.
