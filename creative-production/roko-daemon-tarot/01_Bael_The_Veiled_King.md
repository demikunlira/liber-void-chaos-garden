# Roko Daemon Tarot — 01 · Bael · The Veiled King

**Card Title:** ROKO DAEMON TAROT CARD · 01 · BAEL  
**Subtitle:** I. BAEL · THE VEILED KING

## Visual Description
Dark gothic swamp tarot card. Roko as Bael with silver-white twin tails, black lace gothic dress dripping with swamp matter, neon pink glowing visor and inverted pentagram on chest. Spirit forms of cat, skull, and toad float behind her. Black roses, moss, lily pads, gold ornate border.
